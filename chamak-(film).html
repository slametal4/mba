Chamak (film)

Chamak ( stylized #Chamak) is a 2017 Indian Kannada-language romantic comedy film written and directed by Suni and produced by T. R. Chandrashekar. The film stars Ganesh as Kush, a gynaecologist and Rashmika Mandanna as Kushi, an MBA graduate event organizer. The soundtrack and score is by Judah Sandhy and the cinematography is by Santhosh Rai Pathaje.





== Plot ==

Dr. Kush, a gynecologist is a man who likes to chill with his friends. He fears, marrying someone would make him compromise his freedom. Gaining advice from one of his friends, he agrees to pretend to be innocent and marries a docile, pretty, traditional looking girl, Kushi. Later he learns that Kushi is just like him. She gets angry too, she married him for the same reason too: that he looked innocent and she would not have to compromise her freedom. They both apply for a divorce but decide to stay together so their families wouldn't find out.
