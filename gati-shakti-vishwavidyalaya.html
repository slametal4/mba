Gati Shakti Vishwavidyalaya

Gati Shakti Vishwavidyalaya (GSV), is a Central University located in Vadodara, Gujarat, India. GSV is India's first Transportation and Logistics University, which was created by an Act of Parliament in August 2022.





== History ==

In 2017, cabinet of India led by the prime minister Narendra Modi approved the setting up of India's first university exclusively focused on transport-related education, multidisciplinary research and training, called National Rail and Transportation Institute (NRTI). It was established in 2018.

In 2022, NRTI was subsumed by Gati Shakti Vishwavidyalaya.
