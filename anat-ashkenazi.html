Anat Ashkenazi

Anat Ashkenazi is the CFO of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary Google. She had previously worked at Eli Lilly and Company since 2001, finishing as CFO there.





== Early life and career ==

An Israeli-American, Ashkenazi earned a degree in finance and economics from the Hebrew University of Jerusalem and an MBA from Tel Aviv University, according to Business Insider. Ynet News reports she studied accounting and economics at the Hebew University of Jerusalem. She worked in the financial services sector in Israel, including for Ma'alot Standard & Poor's and Bank Hapoalim.
