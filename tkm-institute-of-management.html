TKM Institute of Management

The TKM Institute of Management (TIM) is a business management school in Kerala. TIM which is governed by TKM College Trust offers an MBA program which is approved by AICTE and affiliated to University of Kerala. This MBA program is accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA). Recruiters to TIM include Nestle, Heinz, ITC, Microsoft, Axis Bank, HDFC Bank, Federal Bank, South Indian Bank, Colgate, Nerolac Paints. The average salary of MBA participants in 2018-19 stood at Rs.3.6 lakhs and the highest salary was Rs.9.6 lakhs (CTC). TKM Institute of Management has around 2000 alumni spread across the world with 24 batches of MBA graduates.
